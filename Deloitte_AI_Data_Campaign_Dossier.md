## Strong Public Project Signals

### 1. Policy Navigator / Case Management

Deloitte describes a government/social-services policy navigator where workers searched complex policies in natural language, reducing lookup time from hours to seconds. This is very close to the ConsultIQ pattern: messy policy/workflow context, governed retrieval, business-user update path, and scalable adoption.

Campaign relevance:
- ConsultIQ can be positioned as a governed policy/workflow navigator for consulting operations.
- A future add-on could make ConsultIQ more visibly "policy navigator" than "chatbot."

### 2. GenAI Prototype For Defence / Global Security

Deloitte describes building a customized ChatGPT-like GenAI solution for document analysis and AI assistant tasks for a major international alliance, including agile phases, client feedback, and a three-day hackathon to co-develop user stories. The first phase delivered a working prototype covering 40 of 60 user stories.

Campaign relevance:
- This validates the value of a working prototype plus case-study narrative.
- ConsultIQ should emphasize user stories, workflow coverage, controls, and adoption path.

### 3. The Agentic Supply Chain

Deloitte's April 16, 2026 paper says the agentic shift is structural: agentic AI removes certain decisions from the human queue, low-code agent platforms make building less of the hard part, and the remaining challenge is workflow redesign, decision rights, and leadership ownership.

Campaign relevance:
- This is one of the best Deloitte-specific hooks for a new mini-project.
- A practical demo could be "Agentic Supply Chain Exception Desk": agents triage supplier risk, procurement blockers, compliance exceptions, and escalation paths.

### 4. Trustworthy AI / AI Governance

Deloitte's AI pages repeatedly emphasize trust, risk, governance, privacy, ethics, controls, data stewardship, human-in-the-loop validation, and moving beyond shadow AI.

Campaign relevance:
- ConsultIQ's deterministic compliance layer is valuable because it shows controls in code rather than relying on the model.
- This is the safest way to differentiate from generic AI demos.

## Recommended Proof Assets

### Reuse ConsultIQ, but reframe it for Deloitte

Do not rebuild from scratch first. The fastest campaign asset is a Deloitte-specific case-study paragraph/page.

Suggested title:

**ConsultIQ: governed AI workflow prototype for enterprise transformation**

Suggested one-liner:

> ConsultIQ turns an ambiguous consulting problem into a governed AI workflow: AI helps generate the work, while software controls enforce compliance, structure, evidence, and review.

What to emphasize:
- workshop-to-workflow thinking
- proof of concept / pilot logic
- governance framework
- deterministic compliance controls
- executive-ready output
- cross-functional handoff between business and technical users
- responsible AI and auditability

### Optional Deloitte-specific mini-project

If there is time, build one small companion demo instead of a whole new app.

Best option:

**Agentic Policy Navigator / Exception Desk**

Concept:
- Input: messy policy, client request, or operational exception
- Agent/tool layer: classify issue, retrieve rules, draft action path, identify risks, route to human approval
- Governance: deterministic checks, audit trail, review gate, source references
- Output: executive-ready brief + workflow trace

Why it matches Deloitte:
- Mirrors policy navigation case study.
- Mirrors AI & Data Transformation job language.
- Mirrors Trustworthy AI.
- Mirrors agentic enterprise theme.

Second option:

**AI ROI / Governance Control Tower**

Concept:
- Input: proposed AI use case
- Output: baseline metric, value hypothesis, adoption risk, governance checklist, responsible owner, pilot score

Why it matches Deloitte:
- Mirrors the "move from experimentation to measurable impact" theme.
- Helps show business judgment, not only coding.

Third option:

**ServiceNow NowAssist-Style Workflow Agent**

Concept:
- A generic ticket/workflow triage agent that classifies requests, proposes automation, drafts knowledge-base articles, and escalates risks.

Why it matches Deloitte:
- Supports the NowAssist role, but only worth building if targeting that posting seriously.
